The Current State

Understanding Fast International & Saruest

Dr. Tarig Mohy Aldin leads two businesses we're proposing to bring under one digital foundation: Fast International, the family's flagship group since 1982, and Saruest Investment, its alternative energy arm since 2015. Both are established, credible, and currently invisible online.

Fast International

Founded in 1982 in Port Sudan, Fast International has grown into one of Sudan's leading diversified groups across nine divisions — pharmaceuticals, transport & clearance, agriculture, medical equipment, food processing, and newer moves into mining, power & energy, electricity, and education. The pharmaceutical division alone, established 1993, represents international names including Hikma, Novartis, Sandoz, and Medochemie. The group's own motto — "Winning Your Confidence" — is exactly what a credible digital presence should reinforce, and today there is none.

Saruest Investment

Saruest is one of Sudan's leading solar and alternative energy companies, established in 2015, with a decade of experience delivering solutions from 100kW residential systems to national-scale projects. As the authorized agent for Schneider Electric Solar, Luminous Power Technologies, Selex, and Himel, Saruest has the partnerships and track record — it has an existing logo and tagline, "Think Solar, Think Saruest," but no website and an identity that hasn't been refreshed since launch.

The Challenge

Like many companies operating in Sudan today, both businesses run on WhatsApp, Excel spreadsheets, and basic tools. Neither has a website, a real brand system, or professional email on its own domain.

What's Next

Phase 2: CRM & Operations Platform

Once the foundation above is live, there's a natural next step we'd like to preview rather than price today: a custom CRM and ERP platform purpose-built around how both companies actually work — sales pipeline, an AI-powered proposal builder for Saruest's solar quotes, inventory and procurement across Khartoum and Dubai warehouses, and role-based access for the leadership team (Chairman, CEO Dubai, CEO Sudan, COO, BDM) down to sales reps and technicians. Nothing like this exists among regional competitors today. We'll scope and price this as its own proposal once the digital foundation is in place — sequencing matters here, and a brand and website built before the identity exists is exactly the mistake this proposal is designed to avoid repeating in reverse.
