The Current State

Understanding Saruest

Saruest is one of Sudan's leading solar and alternative energy companies established in 2015, with over a decade of experience delivering solutions ranging from 100kW residential systems to 400MW national projects. As the authorized agent for Schneider Electric Solar, Luminous Power Technologies, Selex, and Himel Saruest has the partnerships, expertise, and track record.

Saruest's vision is to become the leading provider of alternative energy power in Sudan and the neighbouring region, from small-scale domestic power solutions right through to large-scale national alternative energy power projects and solutions.

The Challenge

Like many companies operating in Sudan today, Saruest runs its operations on WhatsApp, Excel spreadsheets, and basic tools. There is no website, no efficient CRM and projects management system, and no digital pipeline.
